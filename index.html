About Me

I am Siyuan Gong from Zhejiang University and University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, majoring Electrical and Computer Engineering(ECE), expected to receive the degree of Bachelor of Science in June, 2027.

My research interests primarily focus on developing advanced foundation models for graphs. Currently, I am working on pre-training a general graph foundation model using a large corpus of graph data, under the guidance of Professor Yang Yang.
