Experience

  • Graph-Based In-Context Learning Enhancement

    • One of the most important experiments of my life! It ignited my passion for research and made me rethink my future plans. Our work is currently submitted to KDD 2025. I contributed as the second author, helping to refine the idea, conduct experiments, and write the paper. For a detailed reflection and summary of this experience, check out my

    blog.

  • iGEM Competition

    • An amazing interdisciplinary adventure! I helped apply neural network and Python scripts to batch process biological images and data. I also contributed to build a math model to simulate gene interactions. Our goal? Making cosmetics cheap and safe (which girl doesn’t care about that, especially when it’s for skincare^ω^).

  • COMAP Math Modeling Competition

    • It was a valuable experience that taught me how to tackle the unfamiliar and uncertain. I had to quickly grasp essential knowledge and identify the most important aspects. Working with a team of three required managing disagreements, learning to persuade others, and finding compromises. Overall, brilliant!

    • I assisted elderly patients with electronic registration at a hospital, reflecting on technology's role in progress and digital divides. I also tutored a middle school student from Xinjiang Province in physics. It broadens my perspective of the world and makes me grateful for what I have. Let’s work together and make a little positive impact.o(≧v≦)o
