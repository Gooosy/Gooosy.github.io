About Me

Hi, I am Siyuan Gong from Zhejiang University and University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, majoring Electrical and Computer Engineering(ECE), expected to receive the degree of Bachelor of Science in June, 2027.

My research interests primarily focus on developing advanced foundation models for graphs. Currently, I am working on pre-training a general graph foundation model using a large corpus of graph data, under the guidance of Professor Yang Yang.

Experience

  • COMAP Math Modeling Competition

    • In the COMAP Math Modeling Competition, our team progressed from basic beginnings to a rapid learning and adaptation journey, sharpening our ability to quickly identify and absorb critical concepts. The competition taught me the art of collaboration – listening respectfully to team members, effectively presenting my ideas, and embracing the spirit of mutual understanding and compromise.

  • Volunteer Work

    • I greatly cherish engaging in volunteer work. During the summer before starting university, I volunteered at a hospital to assist elderly patients with electronic registration machines. This experience taught me about the dual role of my field of study, computer engineering, in fostering societal progress and convenience, alongside broadening digital division. Itprompted me to reflect on how to optimize technology for the benefit of a broader population. Additionally, I engaged in online tutoring, teaching physics to a middle school student from Xinjiang every weekend. This teaching experience was not only an application of my knowledge but also a mutual learning process. It fostered in me a deep gratitude for the educational resources I have and motivated me to fully utilize and diligently pursue my studies.

  • Sports

    • In high school, I earned 5th place in the 1500-meter race and later secured 2nd in the 800 meters at my university's sports meet. Additionally, I participated in team events like relay races and tug-of-war. Sports have provided me with more than just a way to relax and stay fit.
